# Constitutional Governance

> **Faithful note:** the harness treats this as **optional**, added *after* recurring patterns appear —
> not on day one. We include a minimal version as a teaching building block. It codifies, at a higher
> altitude, the principles already in `CLAUDE.md`. When these articles and `CLAUDE.md` conflict, these
> articles win.

## Article I — Plan-First
No non-trivial change (>3 files, >30 min, or multi-step) begins without a written plan saved to disk
and approved by the human. See `.claude/rules/plan-first-workflow.md`.

## Article II — Quality Gate
Nothing is committed below a quality score of **80/100**. Shipping (presenting / a PR) requires
**90+**. See `.claude/rules/quality-gates.md`.

## Article III — Verification Standard
A task is "done" only after the output has been compiled / rendered / run and checked. No success is
claimed from imagination. See `.claude/rules/verification-protocol.md`.

## Article IV — Adversarial Review
Quality is judged by **specialists**, not a generalist. A **critic that cannot edit** finds faults; a
**fixer that cannot approve its own work** repairs them; the critic re-audits until a clean pass.

## Article V — Memory & Provenance
Decisions are written to disk (plans, session notes, the memory drawers) before context compaction can
erase them. Git records *what*; the logs record *why*.

## Article VI — Audience Fidelity  (project-specific)
Every public artifact frames agentic AI for non-technical methodologists per
`.claude/rules/audience-framing.md`: delegation over productivity, values first, honesty over magic.

## Amendments
A new standing rule is added only after a pattern recurs ~3–7 times. Prefer a **path-scoped rule**
(loads only when relevant) over bloating `CLAUDE.md` or these articles.
